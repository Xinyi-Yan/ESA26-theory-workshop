The discrete logistic growth model is: Nt+1 = Nt + rNt(1 − Nt/K). At each time step, the population changes according to a density-dependent per-capita growth rate. Here, r is the intrinsic growth rate for the continuous exponential growth (i.e. r + 1 = &lambda in the discrete exponential growth model), while (1 − N/K) reduces that growth rate as the population approaches the carrying capacity K.
